About Us

Family owned and operated, El Paso Tacos & Tequila is proud to bring the lovely city of Victoria our family's Tex-Mex recipes and all your favorite tequilas. Our family friendly restaurant offers a kids menu and vegetarian options. We love to host birthday parties and any other special events in our upstairs party area. Check out our events tab for live music every week!
